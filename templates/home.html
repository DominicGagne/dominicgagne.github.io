My Research

My Graduate research is centered around User Modelling in Software Applications, and lies within the domain of Human Computer Interaction.

I aim to study the effects of User Modelling in Post-Secondary Academic settings, and how such an implementation technique might differ from more traditional marketing-oriented User Modelling.
